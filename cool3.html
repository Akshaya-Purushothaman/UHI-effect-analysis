Reason and Solutions for UHI

Cluster Summary

  • Cluster 0: High Building Density (~70%), Low Green Area (~60%), Low Water Bodies (~30%), UHI Index: Slightly Negative (-0.32)
  • Cluster 1: Very Low Building Density (~40%), High Green Area (~71%), High Water Bodies (~59%), UHI Index: Very Negative (-0.98)
  • Cluster 2: Moderate Building Density (~56%), Very Low Green Area (~29%), Moderate Water Bodies (~26%), UHI Index: Positive (1.78)

Recommendations for Each Cluster

Cluster 0: High Building Density, Low Green Area, Low Water Bodies

Increase Green Spaces: Develop more parks, plant trees, and create community gardens.
Reason: Enhancing green coverage can reduce heat absorption and increase cooling.
Introduce Cool Roofing Materials: Use reflective or cool roofing materials to reduce heat absorption from roofs.
Reason: These materials help lower surface temperatures and reduce heat gain.
Implement Green Roofs and Walls: Encourage or mandate green roofs and vertical gardens in new buildings.
Reason: These can help absorb heat and improve insulation.
Increase Water Features: Develop small water bodies or fountains in public spaces.
Reason: Water bodies can enhance local cooling and increase humidity.

Cluster 1: Low Building Density, High Green Area, High Water Bodies

Maintain Current Conditions: Continue to maintain and expand existing green spaces and water bodies.
Reason: The high green area and water bodies are contributing to a very low UHI effect.
Promote Sustainable Practices: Encourage sustainable urban practices and green building certifications.
Reason: Maintaining high green coverage and water bodies will sustain low UHI effects.

Cluster 2: Moderate Building Density, Very Low Green Area, Moderate Water Bodies

Increase Green Area: Introduce green roofs, urban parks, and increase tree planting.
Reason: Significant improvement in UHI index is needed, and increasing green space will help mitigate heat.
Use Cool Materials: Implement cool pavements and reflective building materials.
Reason: These materials can reduce heat absorption from surfaces and buildings.
Enhance Water Features: Improve and expand water bodies and introduce more water features.
Reason: More water bodies can provide cooling benefits and enhance local humidity.
